Who to contact

For the right type of enquiry

Different situations need different responses — here's who to contact for each type of enquiry.

I've been referred and waiting to hear

Please wait — we contact every patient once we've reviewed the referral. If your symptoms worsen urgently, contact your GP.

I have an appointment booked

To reschedule or ask a question about your appointment, call us at (03) 548 XXXX — Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm.

It's a medical emergency

Call 111 or go to your nearest emergency department. Our clinic is not an emergency service and cannot provide urgent care outside scheduled appointments.

GP sending a referral

Send by letter, fax, or electronic referral to our Nelson clinic. See our GP information page for full details and what to include.

Billing or insurance questions

Call or email our admin team at the Nelson clinic — (03) 548 XXXX or admin@topofthesouthcardiology.co.nz.

Video or phone appointment

For follow-up and results, remote consultations are available. Ask our team when they contact you to book, or call us to discuss the options.

Before you arrive

What to expect
on the day

  • Arrive 10 minutes early to complete any paperwork and find parking.
  • Bring a list of your current medications and your NHI number.
  • Bring any previous test results or ECGs you have access to.
  • You're welcome to bring a support person — please let us know in advance so we can prepare the room.
  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing — particularly if you're having an echo or ECG test.
  • Don't stop any medications unless specifically advised to by our team or your GP.
