About the practice

Specialist cardiology, delivered personally

Top of the South Cardiology has been providing specialist cardiac services to the Nelson and Marlborough regions for over two decades. We are a small, close-knit team — which means consistent care from people who know you, rather than a different face at every appointment.

Our cardiologists have experience across the full spectrum of cardiac conditions, from initial assessment and investigation through to complex interventional procedures in our purpose-built catheterisation laboratory.

We hold clinics in both Nelson and Marlborough, and are committed to making expert cardiac care accessible to everyone in the region — without the need to travel to Christchurch or Wellington.

Board-certified specialists

All of our cardiologists hold Fellowship of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians (FRACP) with a cardiology subspecialty.

Committed to this region

Our team has deep roots in the Nelson-Marlborough community. We're not visiting specialists — we live here, and we plan to continue serving this community for many years to come.

Ongoing learning & professional development

We maintain active involvement in professional development, peer review, and regional health networks to ensure our practice reflects current evidence and best practice.

Affiliations & accreditation

Who we work with

Nelson Marlborough Health

We work closely with Nelson Marlborough Health, providing specialist cardiac services to both privately referred and publicly funded patients across the region.

Southern Cross Affiliated Provider

We are an affiliated provider with Southern Cross Health Society, meaning most members can access our services without pre-authorisation for standard consultations and investigations.

RACP & CSANZ Members

Our cardiologists are Fellows of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians and members of the Cardiac Society of Australia and New Zealand.
