What we offer

A full range of cardiac investigations, close to home

From your first appointment through to diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up — our team handles every step of your cardiac care locally.

Outpatient tests Angiography suite

CT Coronary Angiography & Calcium Scoring

Non-invasive imaging to assess coronary artery disease risk. Often the first step in a cardiac work-up.

Echocardiography

Ultrasound imaging of the heart to evaluate structure, valve function, and pumping efficiency.

24-Hour & Patch ECG Monitoring

Extended heart rhythm monitoring — from a standard Holter to a waterproof patch worn for weeks at a time.
